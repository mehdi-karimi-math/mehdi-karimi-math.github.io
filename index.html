About me

I am a Postdoctoral Fellow at the Department of Combinatorics and Optimization, University of Waterloo, working with Dr. Levent Tuncel. My research in the last few years has been on designing optimization algorithms, creating my DDS code for convex optimization problems, and studying sum-of-squares techniques and their connections with semidefinite programing. You can read more about my research here.

I love teaching, specially performing new methods of learning and teaching in classrooms. I have completed Fundamentals of University Teaching and Certificate of University Teaching programs. My Project was about implementing Peer-Instruction in mathematics.

Research Interests

  • Mathematical programming
  • Convex analysis
  • Optimization software and applications
  • Sum-of-squares techniques
  • Semidefinite programming
  • Interior-point methods
